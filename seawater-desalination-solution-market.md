<p><p>The seawater desalination market comprises several technologies, primarily including Reverse Osmosis (RO) and Multi-Effect Distillation (MED). RO utilizes semipermeable membranes to separate salt and impurities from seawater, making it energy-efficient and widely adopted. MED, on the other hand, employs multiple stages of evaporation and condensation, suitable for large-scale operations but generally more energy-intensive. Other technologies in the market include nanofiltration and Electrodialysis, each offering unique advantages and suited for specific applications in desalination processes.</p></p>
