<p><p>The transparent film dressings market includes two primary types: transparent wound dressings and transparent self-adhesive dressings. Transparent wound dressings serve as protective barriers over wounds, allowing visibility while providing moisture retention, promoting healing, and preventing infection. Transparent self-adhesive dressings, on the other hand, are specifically designed with an adhesive layer that allows for easy application directly onto the skin, ensuring secure placement and minimizing friction. Both types enhance patient comfort and simplify wound care management in clinical settings.</p></p>
