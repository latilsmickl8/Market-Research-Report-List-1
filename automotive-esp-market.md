<p><strong>The Automotive ESP Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Channel</li><li>3-Channel</li><li>4-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Stability Program (ESP) market is categorized into three types: 2-Channel, 3-Channel, and 4-Channel systems. </p><p>A 2-Channel system typically controls the front wheels, suitable for basic vehicles, while a 3-Channel system adds control for one rear wheel, enhancing stability in moderate conditions. The 4-Channel system monitors and adjusts the braking of each wheel independently, providing optimal performance and safety in diverse driving scenarios. As vehicle technology progresses, the 4-Channel system is increasingly preferred for advanced safety features.</p></p>
