<p><p>The flammable gas sensor market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Molecular Property Spectrometer Sensors analyze gas composition by measuring molecular properties, providing high accuracy. Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Sensors detect gas concentration based on infrared absorption, favored for their reliability. Catalytic Gas Sensors utilize chemical reactions to identify the presence of flammable gases, commonly used in industrial settings. Other sensors may include photoionization detectors and electrochemical sensors, broadening the market’s scope by catering to diverse requirements across various industries.</p></p>
