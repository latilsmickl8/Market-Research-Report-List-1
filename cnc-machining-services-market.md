<p><p>CNC machining services encompass various types, each tailored for specific manufacturing needs. Turning involves rotating the workpiece against a cutting tool to shape it, while milling utilizes stationary cutters to remove material from a moving workpiece. Drilling creates holes, while boring enlarges existing ones for precision. Broaching uses a toothed tool for complex shapes, and sawing involves cutting materials into desired sizes. Additionally, "Others" may include specialized processes like grinding or electrical discharge machining, catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
