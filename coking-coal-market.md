<p><p>The coking coal market comprises several types, each serving distinct purposes in steel production. Hard Coking Coals (HCC) are high-quality coals essential for metallurgical processes, offering superior strength. Medium Coking Coal has slightly lower caking properties but is still suitable for steelmaking. Semi-soft Coking Coal (SSCC) is used in blends to lower costs while maintaining quality. Pulverized Coal Injection (PCI) Coal is injected into blast furnaces to enhance efficiency and reduce coke consumption, optimizing overall production processes.</p></p>
