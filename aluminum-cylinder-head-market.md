<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting Aluminum Alloy</li><li>Low Pressure Casting Aluminum Alloy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum cylinder head market primarily includes two types of casting processes: gravity casting and low-pressure casting. Gravity casting utilizes gravitational force to fill molds with molten aluminum, offering consistency and precision, suitable for complex designs. In contrast, low-pressure casting involves maintaining a controlled low-pressure environment to push the molten aluminum into molds, allowing for high-quality surfaces and reduced porosity. Both methods are essential for producing durable, lightweight cylinder heads that enhance engine performance in automotive applications.</p></p>
