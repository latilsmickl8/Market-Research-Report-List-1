<p><p>The bulk acoustic wave (BAW) sensor market is primarily segmented into three types: Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM), Thin Film Bulk Acoustic Resonators (TFBAR), and Film Bulk Acoustic Resonators (FBAR) with Surface-Mount Resonators (SMR). QCM sensors are widely used for mass sensing applications due to their precision. TFBARs offer enhanced performance in telecommunications, while FBAR-SMR devices excel in high-frequency applications, particularly in RF and microwave devices, providing efficient bandwidth utilization and miniaturization benefits in various industries.</p></p>
