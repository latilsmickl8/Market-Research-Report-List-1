<p><ul><li>Fluoroprotein Foam</li><li>Protein Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Protein Firefighting Foam market comprises primarily two types: Fluoroprotein Foam and Protein Foam. Fluoroprotein Foam incorporates fluorinated surfactants, enhancing its effectiveness in suppressing flammable liquid fires and providing improved water resistance. It is commonly used in industrial settings and aviation firefighting. Protein Foam, derived from hydrolyzed proteins, offers suitable fire suppression capabilities but lacks the enhanced performance properties of fluorinated variants. Its applications include general firefighting and scenarios where environmental concerns hinder the use of fluorinated products.</p></p>
