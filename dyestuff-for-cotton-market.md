<p><ul><li>Reactive Dyes</li><li>Disperse Dyes</li><li>Direct Dyes</li><li>Sulfur Dyes</li><li>Optical Brighteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dyestuff market for cotton primarily includes reactive dyes, disperse dyes, direct dyes, sulfur dyes, and optical brighteners. Reactive dyes bond chemically with cotton fibers, offering vibrant colors and wash durability. Disperse dyes are used for synthetic blends, providing bright colors and good fastness. Direct dyes offer ease of application for cellulosic fabrics but may have lower fastness. Sulfur dyes are economical, providing dark shades with good wash fastness. Optical brighteners enhance color brightness and whiteness in cotton textiles.</p></p>
