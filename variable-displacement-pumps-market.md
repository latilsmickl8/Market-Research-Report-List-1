<p><strong>The Variable Displacement Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable Displacement Piston Pump</li><li>Variable Displacement Vane Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The variable displacement pumps market consists primarily of two types: variable displacement piston pumps and variable displacement vane pumps. Variable displacement piston pumps adjust fluid flow by altering piston stroke length, offering high efficiency and pressure capabilities, suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, variable displacement vane pumps use rotating vanes to change displacement, providing smoother flow and lower pulsation, making them ideal for hydraulic systems. Both types are crucial in industries requiring adaptable fluid power solutions.</p></p>
