<p><p>The EV charging solution market includes two primary types: Level 2 and Level 3 charging solutions. Level 2 charging uses a 240-volt supply, typically suitable for home and public installations, offering faster charging times of 4-10 hours. In contrast, Level 3 charging, also known as DC fast charging, utilizes high-voltage systems to provide rapid charging in 20-30 minutes, ideal for commercial locations and long-distance travel. Both types are crucial for expanding EV adoption and infrastructure development.</p></p>
