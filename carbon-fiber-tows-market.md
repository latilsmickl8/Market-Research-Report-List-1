<p><p>The carbon fiber tows market is primarily divided into two types: PAN-based and pitch-based carbon fibers. PAN-based carbon fiber, derived from polyacrylonitrile, is widely used due to its excellent mechanical properties, thermal stability, and versatility in applications ranging from aerospace to sports equipment. In contrast, pitch-based carbon fiber, produced from petroleum or coal tar pitch, offers superior thermal conductivity and is often utilized in applications requiring high electrical conductivity, like batteries and composites. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
