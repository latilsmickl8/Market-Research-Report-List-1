<p><p>The Controlled Descent Device (CDD) market can be segmented into two main types based on operation: manual and automatic. Manual operation requires users to actively control their descent, offering flexibility and immediate response during emergencies. In contrast, automatic operation utilizes pre-programmed systems to regulate descent speed and direction, enhancing safety for users who may lack training or experience. Both types cater to various user needs, ensuring effective deployment in situations like high-rise evacuations or technical rescues.</p></p>
