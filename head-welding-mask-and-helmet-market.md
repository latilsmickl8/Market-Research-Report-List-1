<p><p>The head welding mask and helmet market comprises two primary types: passive type and auto-darkening type. Passive type helmets feature a fixed shaded lens, offering consistent protection but requiring manual removal to view. In contrast, auto-darkening helmets have sensors that detect welding arcs, automatically adjusting the lens shade for optimal visibility during welding. This innovation enhances convenience and safety by allowing welders to maintain their protective gear while working, catering to diverse user preferences and improving overall efficiency in welding tasks.</p></p>
