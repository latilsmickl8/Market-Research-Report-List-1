<p><p>Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based VMI offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of access, enabling real-time collaboration between vendors and retailers through internet connectivity. This model reduces upfront costs and maintenance burdens. Conversely, on-premises VMI software is installed directly on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and security but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing IT support. Both types facilitate efficient inventory management and enhanced supply chain collaboration.</p></p>
