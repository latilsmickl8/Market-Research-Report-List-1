<p><p>The DNA Gene Chip market comprises various types, primarily cDNA and oDNA. cDNA (Complementary DNA) chips are designed to measure gene expression levels by hybridizing to complementary RNA transcripts, making them essential for studying cellular function. On the other hand, oDNA (Oligonucleotide DNA) chips utilize short, synthesized DNA sequences for high-resolution analysis of genetic variations and polymorphisms. Both types play crucial roles in genomics, diagnostics, and personalized medicine, facilitating advanced research in genetics and molecular biology.</p></p>
