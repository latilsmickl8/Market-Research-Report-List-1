<p><strong>The Home Sleep Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home sleep monitoring equipment is categorized into contact and contactless types. Contact devices require physical attachment to the user, such as wearable sensors or headbands, which track various sleep parameters through direct skin interaction. In contrast, contactless devices monitor sleep patterns without physical contact, utilizing technologies like radar or camera-based systems. These systems analyze movement and breathing patterns from a distance, offering convenience and comfort. Both types aim to improve sleep quality by providing valuable insights into sleep behavior.</p></p>
