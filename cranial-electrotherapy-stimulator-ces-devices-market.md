<p><p>Cranial Electrotherapy Stimulator (CES) devices, such as P-Stim and E-pulse, are designed to deliver low-level electrical impulses to the brain, primarily for treating anxiety, depression, and insomnia. P-Stim utilizes transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation via auricular electrodes, providing non-invasive neuromodulation. In contrast, E-pulse incorporates programmable features for personalized treatment, enhancing user engagement. Both types aim to promote relaxation and improve mental well-being, making them popular alternatives to pharmaceutical interventions in mental health therapy.</p></p>
