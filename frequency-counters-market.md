<p><p>Frequency counters are specialized instruments used to measure the frequency of signals, and they come in various types catering to different applications. Amp-clamp adapters enhance measurement capabilities, while dual counters allow simultaneous frequency assessments. Embedded frequency counters are integrated into systems for real-time monitoring. Handheld counters offer portability for field use, whereas rate counters measure frequency over time, and totalizer counters accumulate frequency data for long-term tracking. Each type serves unique needs across industries like telecommunications, electronics, and manufacturing.</p></p>
