<p><p>Tire testing services encompass various market types, including tensile, tear, adhesion, compression, and fatigue tests. The tensile test evaluates a tire's ability to withstand stretching forces, while the tear test assesses its resistance to tearing. Adhesion tests measure the bond strength between tire components. Compression tests gauge the material's behavior under compressive loads, and fatigue tests simulate repeated stress cycles to predict lifespan and performance under real-world conditions. Each test ensures tire safety, durability, and performance standards are met.</p></p>
