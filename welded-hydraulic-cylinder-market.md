<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li><li>Multi-Stage/ Tandem</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welded hydraulic cylinder market consists of various types tailored for specific applications. Single-acting cylinders use hydraulic pressure to extend but rely on external forces for retraction. Double-acting cylinders provide force in both directions, offering more controlled motion. Multi-stage or tandem cylinders incorporate multiple stages to achieve extended stroke lengths while maintaining compact sizes, making them ideal for applications with space constraints. Each type serves distinct operational needs, enhancing efficiency and performance in hydraulic systems across industries.</p></p>
