The market consists of three types: single-axis scanners, which move in one direction to achieve linear positioning; two-axis scanners, allowing for movement in both horizontal and vertical planes, enabling more complex scan patterns; and triaxial scanners, which add a third axis for advanced applications requiring rotational movement. Each type caters to specific applications in industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and telecommunications.</p></p>
