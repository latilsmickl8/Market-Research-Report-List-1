<p><p>The key-operated switch market comprises various types, including 1-position, 2-position, 3-position, and 4-position switches. A 1-position switch offers a single function, typically ON or OFF. A 2-position switch provides two distinct functions, such as ON and OFF or two separate settings. A 3-position switch can toggle among three functions, allowing for more complex control. The 4-position switch offers four different states, enabling even greater versatility in applications, catering to diverse operational needs across industries.</p></p>
