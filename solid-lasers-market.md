<p><p>The solid lasers market is primarily divided into two types: pulse type and continuous type. Pulse type lasers emit short bursts of light, ideal for applications requiring high peak power and precision, such as laser cutting and medical procedures. In contrast, continuous type lasers provide a steady beam of light, suitable for applications like material processing, welding, and telecommunications, where constant output is essential. Both types cater to diverse industries, driving innovation and technological advancements in laser technology.</p></p>
