<p><p>The Micro Camera Laparoscope market can be divided into two primary types: single-hole and porous laparoscopes. Single-hole laparoscopes use a single incision for entry, offering less trauma and quicker recovery times. This type is ideal for minimally invasive procedures. Porous laparoscopes, on the other hand, feature a design that allows for gas exchange and tissue interaction, enhancing visibility and reducing the risk of complications. Both types cater to the growing demand for advanced surgical techniques and patient-centered approaches.</p></p>
