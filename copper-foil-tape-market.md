<p><p>The copper foil tape market is primarily segmented into two types: conductive tapes and adhesive tapes. Conductive tapes are utilized for their electrical conductivity in applications such as grounding, shielding, and EMI/RFI protection. Adhesive tapes, on the other hand, feature a sticky backing for easy application and adhere to various surfaces while providing conductive properties. Both types are widely employed in electronics, automotive, and construction industries, catering to needs like circuit repair, anti-static solutions, and thermal management.</p></p>
