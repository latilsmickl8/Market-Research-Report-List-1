<p><strong>The Dry Etching Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP)</li><li>Capacitive Coupled Plasma (CCP)</li><li>Reactive Ion Etching (RIE)</li><li>Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry etching equipment market features several distinct types, each utilizing different plasma technologies. Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) provides high ion density for precise etching, while Capacitive Coupled Plasma (CCP) offers lower ion density with moderate control. Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) combines chemical and physical etching for versatility, whereas Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) enables intricate structures with vertical profiles. Other methods include various hybrid techniques that enhance etching capabilities, catering to diverse applications across semiconductor and materials industries.</p></p>
