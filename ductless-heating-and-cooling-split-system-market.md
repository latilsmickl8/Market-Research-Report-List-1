<p><p>Ductless heating and cooling split systems include mini split systems, multi split systems, and Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) systems. Mini splits consist of a single outdoor unit paired with one or more indoor units, ideal for individual rooms. Multi splits have one outdoor unit connected to multiple indoor units, offering more flexibility for larger spaces. VRF systems allow for simultaneous heating and cooling across different zones, optimizing energy efficiency and comfort in larger buildings, catering to diverse climate control needs.</p></p>
