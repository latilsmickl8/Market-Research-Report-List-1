<p><p>The Power Station Boiler market includes three primary types: coal-fired, oil-fired, and gas-fired boilers. Coal-fired boilers utilize coal as fuel, offering high energy output and efficiency, though they face environmental concerns. Oil-fired boilers burn fuel oil, providing flexibility in fuel choice but often incurring higher operational costs. Gas-fired boilers are the most environmentally friendly option, using natural gas for cleaner combustion and lower emissions, making them increasingly popular in modern energy generation systems. Each type serves distinct energy needs and regulatory considerations.</p></p>
