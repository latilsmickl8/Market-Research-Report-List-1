<p><ul><li>Passive Attenuator</li><li>Active Attenuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The attenuator market comprises two main types: passive and active attenuators. Passive attenuators utilize resistive elements to reduce signal strength without requiring power, making them simple, reliable, and cost-effective for applications like audio and RF signal processing. In contrast, active attenuators incorporate electronic components that require power to adjust signal levels dynamically, offering better performance in managing wide dynamic ranges and minimizing distortion. Both types serve crucial roles in telecommunications, broadcasting, and various electronic devices.</p></p>
