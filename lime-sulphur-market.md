<p><p>The Lime Sulphur market includes two primary types: Soluble Liquid Concentrate (SL) and Suspension Concentrate (SC). SL is a water-soluble formulation that allows for easy mixing and application, making it ideal for foliar spraying and soil treatment. In contrast, SC is a formulation where solid particles are suspended in a liquid, providing stability and extended delivery of active ingredients. Both types are used in agriculture for pest control and disease management, enhancing crop health and yield.</p></p>
